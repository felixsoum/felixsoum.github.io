Chicken Clicker does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

Chicken Clicker saves your click count locally on your device so your progress can remain available after closing and reopening the game.

You can reset your click count inside the game using the reset option.

Chicken Clicker does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 or from any other users.

Chicken Clicker does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, social media login, or online tracking services.

6. Data Sharing

Chicken Clicker does not share personal data with any companies, organizations, or individuals.

7. Data Security

Since Chicken Clicker does not collect or transmit personal information, no personal information is stored on our servers.

The only game data stored is the local click count saved on your own device.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Chicken Clicker does not retain personal information.

The local click count remains on your device until:

  • You reset it inside the game
